Destination 1

Kuta Beach, located on the island of Bali, Indonesia, is one of the most famous beach destinations in the world. The beach is situated to the south of Denpasar, the capital of Bali, and offers a captivating blend of natural beauty, excellent surfing waves, and a delightful beach atmosphere.

Destination 2

Nusa Dua Beach, situated in the southern part of Bali, Indonesia, offers an exclusive and tranquil beach experience. Renowned for its soft white sands and crystal-clear waters, this beach has become a popular tourist destination. With stunning natural scenery and a peaceful atmosphere, Nusa Dua Beach provides a serene retreat for visitors seeking the beauty of its gentle shores and clear sea.

Destination 3

Sawarna Beach is located in Sawarna Village, Bayah District, Lebak Regency, in the province of Banten, to the west of the capital city, Jakarta. It is a hidden gem with stunning natural beauty. The beach is renowned for its black sands and enticing waves that attract surfers.

Destination 4

Senggigi Beach is a renowned tourist destination in Lombok, situated on the western coast of the island. While it may not be as extensive as Kuta Beach in Bali, a visit to Senggigi instantly evokes a sense of being in Bali's Kuta Beach.

Destinasi Pantai 5

Bidadari Island is one of the resorts located in the Thousand Islands Regency, Jakarta. Before being named Bidadari Island, it had two other names, namely Sakit Island and Purmerend Island.

Destination 6

The Raja Ampat Islands are an archipelago located off the western tip of the Bird's Head Peninsula of Papua Island. Administratively, this cluster falls under the regency of Raja Ampat and the city of Sorong in the West Papua Province.
